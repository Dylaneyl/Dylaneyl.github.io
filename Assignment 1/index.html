What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

When I first opened the webpage, I encountered a simple orange screen with artistic scribbling unfolding before my eyes. The initial thing that caught my attention was a prompt to enable sound by clicking on this screen. The sound prompt closely followed my cursor, highlighting the significance of audio in the experience. Additionally, the word 'story' on the landing page indicated that I was about to start a visual and auditory journey unlike a typical website in which i was expecting.
